Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. QRKhata is built to protect your financial data and ensure complete transparency in how your information is used.

📱 Data We Collect

Information We Collect

  • Mobile number (for OTP-based login)
  • Transaction and credit (udhaar) records
  • Basic device and usage information
📷 Permissions

Permissions We Use

Camera: Used only for scanning QR codes to record transactions. We do not store images or videos.

Location (Optional): With your consent, we may access your location to improve transaction context, detect nearby shops, and enhance trust and fraud prevention. Location access is optional and can be disabled anytime.

Internet: Required for syncing data and sending notifications.

⚙️ Usage

How We Use Your Data

  • Enable credit tracking and transaction recording
  • Send reminders, settlements, and notifications
  • Improve product experience and performance
🔐 Security

Data Protection

We use secure systems and encryption to protect your data. Your financial activity remains private and protected.

🤝 Sharing

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information may only be shared with trusted services or when required by law.

👤 Your Rights

Your Control

You can request access, updates, or deletion of your data at any time.
